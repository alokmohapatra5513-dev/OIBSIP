Conversion Mechanics

Curious about how the math works? Here are the fundamental formulas used to shift scales.

Celsius to Others

Fahrenheit: (C × 9/5) + 32

Kelvin: C + 273.15

Fahrenheit to Others

Celsius: (F - 32) × 5/9

Kelvin: ((F - 32) × 5/9) + 273.15

Kelvin to Others

Celsius: K - 273.15

Fahrenheit: ((K - 273.15) × 9/5) + 32
